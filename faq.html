Everything you need to know

Have questions? Here you'll find answers to the most common inquiries about our private chef services, catering, events, and cooking classes. If you don't see what you're looking for, feel free to reach out. We’re happy to assist you!

What types of events do you offer?

Chef Claudio offers catering services, small events, and cooking classes. Each service is tailored to meet the client's needs, whether it's a private dinner, a family gathering, or an interactive cooking class.

How does the catering service work?

The catering service includes creating customized menus with fresh, local ingredients. Chef Claudio prepares the dishes on-site or delivers them ready to be served. It’s perfect for exclusive dinners or more casual events.

What dishes can I expect at small events?

Each event is unique, and menus are created based on the guests' tastes and requests. Chef Claudio offers a wide variety of Puglian dishes, from fresh pasta to meats and traditional desserts, providing an authentic experience.

Are cooking classes suitable for everyone?

Yes, cooking classes are designed for all skill levels, from beginners to experts. The classes are fun, interactive, and allow guests to learn how to cook authentic Puglian dishes with fresh, simple ingredients.

How can I book one of the services?

To book a service, you can contact the chef directly via email or through the contact form on the website. Every detail, from the menu to the date, will be agreed upon to ensure a perfect experience.
